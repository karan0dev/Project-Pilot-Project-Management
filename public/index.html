Why ProjectPilot

Everything a team needs to ship on time

No manual status updates. No orphaned tasks. Just a workspace that recalculates itself the moment work changes.

Live project tracking

Progress bars are never set by hand — they're calculated from completed vs. total tasks the instant something changes.

Structured task flows

Priorities, deadlines, and statuses per task, with cascading updates back to the parent project automatically.

Team visibility

See who's overloaded and who has room, with workload charts pulled straight from live assignment data.

Secure by default

JWT-based sessions, salted password hashing, and role guards protect every private route out of the box.
